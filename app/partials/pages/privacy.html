Collection of personal information:

To serve you better and understand your needs and interests, GFD collects, exports and uses your personal information. For example, when you register on our site, order products or services, request information, participate in surveys, or post information, we will ask for personal information to complete these transactions. The personal information you provide to us may include contact information such as your name, age, gender, address, telephone number, financial information such as a credit card number; and other unique information such as user ID’s and passwords, billing and transaction information, product and service preferences and contact preferences.

If you post, comment or share personal information, including photographs, to your public profile, forum, social network or blog please be aware that any personal information may be read, viewed, collected, or used by other users of the GFD's site, and could be used to contact you and send unsolicited messages, or for other purposes. We encourage you to exercise discretion and caution before posting your personal information.

In addition to the information you provide, GFD may also collect information during your visit to our site by means of automatic data collection tools, which include web beacons, cookies, embedded web links and other commonly used information gathering tools. Such tools collect standard information that your browser sends to our website such as your browser type, language, access times and the address of the website form which you arrived at the GFD website. They may also collect information Protocol (IP) address, behavior (i.e. the pages you view, the links you click and other actions you take in connection with the GFD website).

An IP address is a number that is automatically assigned to your computer whenever you are on the Web, allowing Web servers to locate and indentify your computer. Computers use IP addresses to communicate on the Internet, allowing users to browse, communicate and otherwise be susceptible to identity theft.

GFD may use some of the collection tools in connection with certain emails sent from GFD and therefore may collect information in the email.

How we use your information:

The information collected by GFD is used to understand your needs and interests which helps us deliver a consistent, personalized and enjoyable experience. GFD may use your information to:

  • Prevent and detect fraud or other malicious activity;
  • Assist you in completing a transaction;
  • Communicate with you about products and services;
  • Provide service and support;
  • Update you on new matches;

Credit card information is used only for payment processing. Credit card information required to process a credit card decision is not used for any other purpose and will not be kept longer than necessary for providing the service, unless you request that we retain the information for future transactions.

Except as described in this privacy policy, GFD will not share the information you provide to us with third parties without your permission, unless to: (i) respond to duly authorized information requests from police and governmental authorities: (ii) comply with any law, regulation, subpoena, or court order; (iii) help prevent fraud or to enforce or protect the rights and properties of GFD; or (iv) protect the personal safety of GFD employees.
