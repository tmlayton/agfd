About Us

A Great! First Date was founded in Denver, Colorado in late 2011. We have a simple mission: get people on better dates. What does that mean exactly? Great dates happen when there is a mutual attraction and interest. The site is designed to bring out those two qualities in each and every member by focusing on who you are in the present, not your biography.

Our Story

We, Co-founders Rory and Tim, thought online dating should be easier. We thought it should be more natural. We wanted it to work for the people around us. Rory tried it a couple of times with no success. His friends have tried it. His sister has tried it. Tim tried it. Tim's Mom tried it. The whole thing was just so... unnatural. So we returned to first principles. We asked ourselves, how do we connect with people that we don't know? The answer is surprisingly simple: common interests and shared experiences. This is how Rory met his wife, how Tim met his and what A Great! First Date is meant to be.

We're a small startup, intent on focusing entirely on great user experiences and putting the customer first. We accomplish those goals by using the latest technology and talking to our customers every chance we get.
